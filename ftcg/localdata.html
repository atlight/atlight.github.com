Local wiki data for For the Common Good

See this page for information about local wiki data files, plus how to write your own.

Here is a list of "hosted" local wiki data files that can be loaded live within For the Common Good:

  • German Wikipedia (de.wikipedia) contributed by Gunnex
  • English Wikibooks (en.wikibooks) contributed by Adrignola
  • Persian Wikipedia (fa.wikipedia) contributed by Ebraminio
  • French Wikipedia (fr.wikipedia) contributed by Ebe123
  • Korean Wikipedia (ko.wikipedia) contributed by Ebe123
  • Portuguese Wikipedia (pt.wikipedia) contributed by Gunnex
  • Russian Wikipedia (ru.wikipedia) contributed by Rubin16

There are also a couple of miscellaneous local wiki data files available:

  • English Wikipedia (en.wikipedia) (not necessary, since FtCG has this file built into it)
  • English Wikipedia (en.wikipedia) for Move-to-Commons drive

A big thanks to those who have contributed their local wiki data files.

If you would like your local wiki data file listed here, or if you wish to suggest a change to one of the files listed above, please e-mail it to me.
